Rules

General Rules:
- Be sure to highlight/trim your VODs properly so that the run starts shortly after it begins. Submissions with improperly trimmed VODs will be rejected.
- This board uses loadless timing. All submissions​ must​ include both a loadless time and a time with loads. Incomplete submissions will be rejected.
- The load removal tool for LiveSplit can be found here.
- As of July 1st, 2018, all submissions must have LiveSplit with the loadless remover running visible on the VOD. Submissions that do not meet this criteria will be rejected.
- As of July 7th, 2018, fadeouts before and after loads will now be included in loadless timing. The load remover tool has been updated to reflect this change, and it is required that you update it for future submissions. Any submissions after this change that do not use the updated load remover tool will be rejected. All current runs on the board are being updated to reflect the new timing rule.
- General information on loading times can be found here.
- Frames per second (FPS) must be capped at 60 for PC. For that purpose, an FPS counter has to be clearly visible the whole time - you for example can use the Steam internal FPS counter. Any PC submissions that exceed 60 FPS or do not have an FPS counter in the video will be rejected.
- When submitting, please do not use milliseconds (ms). This section is only for Individual Level (IL) runs.
- Altering the game in any unintended ways (mods, custom PC skins, ...) is banned and will result in rejection of the run.
- The selected game on the title screen must be Crash Bandicoot.



Definition:
- Beat the game.

Timing Rules:
- Timing starts as soon as you confirm a NEW GAME on the title screen and ends upon dealing the final hit to Dr. Neo Cortex.
- Runs must be started from the title screen. Submissions with VODs that do not start on the title screen are subject to rejection.
- Runs are timed using a Real Time Attack (RTA) timing convention. This means that they are timed in a single sitting with an external timer, and the timer cannot be paused.
